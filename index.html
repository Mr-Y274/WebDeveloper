Main Tasks

Web designers create and design websites ‚Äî not just to look good, but to be user-friendly and functional. They plan layouts (wireframes or prototypes), focus on usability and accessibility, and make sure that pages look and work correctly on desktops, tablets, and smartphones (responsive design). They also think about performance and loading speed.
